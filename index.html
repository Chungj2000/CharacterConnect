Connecting education and entertainment through words.

❮ ❯

Your favourite word game, reimaged.

CharacterConnect is a turn-based multiplayer word game inspired by Scrabble, where you can test your wits against other players by creating words to score points. Compete with friends in an interactive environement, and ascend the leaderboard. Additional game modes and features coming soon.

About Us

We of Team 12 are a group of Aston University students who first met during a kick-off event, and since then we've been developing on the game you now know as CharacterConnect. Our team is enthusiastic about our development for CharacterConnect, and we hope to share that enjoyment by delivering the best experience to you our players. The premise for CharacterConnect is to foster an entertaining learning experience for the younger audience, through both casual and competitive matches of a game we all know and love that inspired CharacterConnect.
